Automatic Tag Recommendation for Videos Encompassing Semantic Intelligence

Gerard Deepak, Koushik Iyer, Samiksha Shukla

Conference: 2024 IEEE International Conference for Women in Innovation, Technology & Entrepreneurship (ICWITE), Bangalore, India, 2024
Pages: 1-6
DOI: 10.1109/ICWITE59797.2024.10503160

Abstract

The ATRSI framework is a semantic-oriented, AI-powered system for automatic Web video tagging in the era of Web 3.0. It uses TF-IDF, bag-of-words, and Lin similarity to build a semantic network, optimized with red deer optimization and classified using RNN. Semantic intelligence is enhanced through CoSim rank and Morisita’s overlap index, with lightweight graph embeddings for knowledge representation. ATRSI outperforms baseline models in accuracy, precision, and F-measure across recommendation tasks.
